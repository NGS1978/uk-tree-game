Gold label contrast — before / after

The small gold labels (the “Due” kicker, “Due now”, “Often confused with…”) are too light on the game’s pale backgrounds — about 3.4–4.0:1, under the 4.5:1 accessibility minimum for small text. The fix darkens just that gold text to #7A5400 (~6:1). Borders, fills and the big stat numbers keep today’s gold.
