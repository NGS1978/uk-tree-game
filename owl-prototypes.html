UK Tree Game — tawny owl prototypes

Four rough direction-finders (not finished). Each is a tawny owl — dark eyes, no ear tufts, streaked underparts — shown perched and mid-flight. Tell Claude which direction (or mix) to develop.

1 · Naturalistic

A soft, rounded wildlife-illustration owl with gradient shading and a believable facial disc — the most lifelike of the set.

2 · Vintage plate

A 19th-century ornithology-engraving feel on a cream field — restrained, scholarly, antique character.

3 · Storybook

Warm rufous tones, confident shapes and plenty of plumage texture — personality without cuteness; the widest flight pose.

4 · Heraldic emblem

A graphic, symmetric owl crest with a strong silhouette — reads well as a logo/badge.
